Three-Layer Architecture for Agentic Commerce

SCOUT (User Agent)

  • Learns user values, constraints, preferences
  • Represents intent without exposing identity
  • Negotiates on behalf of principal
  • Maintains continuity across interactions
↓

AURA CORE (Broker/Protocol)

  • Discovery coordination (matching Scouts/Beacons)
  • Trust infrastructure (identity, reputation)
  • Transaction orchestration (negotiation protocols)
  • Market navigation (semantic taxonomy)
↓

BEACON (Seller Agent)

  • Broadcasts offerings with rich metadata
  • Responds to agent queries
  • Negotiates terms dynamically
  • Integrates with backend systems

Neutral coordination layer enables trust without platform bias
