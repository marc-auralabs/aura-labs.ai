## Overview

Integrate your WooCommerce store with AURA Core to publish a Beacon to the network. This guide covers setting up the AURA Beacon plugin, synchronizing products and inventory, and managing orders through AURA transactions.

## What You'll Learn

- Installing the AURA Beacon plugin for WooCommerce
- Configuring product feeds and catalog settings
- Syncing inventory levels between WooCommerce and AURA
- Setting up automated pricing and discount rules
- Converting AURA transactions to WooCommerce orders
- Managing order fulfillment and customer communication

## Prerequisites

- WooCommerce store with plugin access
- AURA developer account
- Basic understanding of Beacon

~30 minutes
