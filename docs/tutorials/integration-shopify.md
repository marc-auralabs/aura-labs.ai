## Overview

Connect your Shopify store to AURA Core and deploy a Beacon to reach Scout agents. This integration guide covers syncing products, inventory, and orders between Shopify and your AURA Beacon.

## What You'll Learn

- Installing the AURA Beacon app for Shopify
- Syncing Shopify products to your Beacon catalog
- Mapping Shopify inventory to AURA inventory levels
- Configuring pricing policies and discounts
- Processing AURA transactions into Shopify orders
- Handling fulfillment and tracking

## Prerequisites

- Shopify store with admin access
- AURA developer account
- Basic understanding of Beacon

## Estimated Time

~30 minutes
