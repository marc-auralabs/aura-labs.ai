## Overview

Customize your Scout's behavior and decision-making by configuring detailed preferences. This guide covers setting budget constraints, quality requirements, delivery preferences, and other criteria that guide your Scout's automatic negotiations with Beacons.

## What You'll Learn

- Setting budget limits and price ranges
- Defining product quality and specification requirements
- Configuring delivery timeframes and location preferences
- Setting negotiation constraints and acceptable terms
- Prioritizing competing objectives (price vs. speed vs. quality)
- Updating preferences over time based on needs

- AURA developer account
- Basic understanding of Scout
