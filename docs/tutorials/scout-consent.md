Implement robust consent and privacy controls for Scout agents operating on your behalf. This guide covers managing permissions, data sharing agreements, consent workflows, and ensuring AURA network communications comply with privacy regulations.

- Understanding consent requirements in AURA network communications
- Setting up consent workflows for Scout operations
- Managing data sharing permissions and privacy policies
- Configuring Scout transparency and disclosure settings
- Handling user opt-in and opt-out mechanisms
- Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations
- Auditing Scout actions and consent records
